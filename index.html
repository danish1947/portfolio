About Me

I'm Danish Muhammad Zein, a Graphic Designer and UI/UX Designer based in Tangerang, Indonesia. I turn ideas into clean, purposeful visual experiences — from brand identities and posters to digital products.

Beyond client work, I'm the Founder and Creative Designer behind two brands of my own: Neovate Apparel, a streetwear label, and Morphate Design, a creative design studio. For me, design isn't just a service — it's a way to build things that carry a point of view.
